🎲 Monte Carlo Simulation: Events & Probability

Run thousands of random trials to observe the Law of Large Numbers. As you increase the number of trials, the empirical (observed) frequencies will converge to the theoretical probabilities.

Theoretical vs Empirical:
The solid bars represent the Observed (Empirical) Frequencies generated by your simulated trials.
The dashed line/overlay represents the Theoretical Probabilities calculated using math.

Try running the simulation with 10 trials, then try 10,000 trials. Notice how much closer the results get to the mathematical theory when you simulate more trials!
